üöÄ Getting Started

How do I get started with Pathways?

Getting started is simple! First, sign up for an account using your email or Google. Then, browse available career roles that interest you, select one, and start tracking your progress as you complete tasks and milestones.

What are career roles on Pathways?

Career roles are structured learning paths that guide you through the steps to achieve a specific career goal. Each role includes stages (e.g., Foundation, Intermediate, Advanced), skills to develop, tasks to complete, and curated resources.

Can I follow multiple career roles at the same time?

Yes! You can follow as many roles as you like. Your dashboard will show progress on all your active roles. However, we recommend focusing on 1‚Äì2 primary roles to maintain consistent progress.

Do I need any prior experience to start?

No! Most roles are designed to be beginner-friendly. Each role starts with foundation-level tasks and gradually progresses to advanced topics. Select roles appropriate to your current skill level.

üë§ Account & Profile

How do I update my profile?

Go to your Profile page from the navigation menu. There, you can edit your name, bio, preferred field, notification preferences, and upload a profile photo.

Can I change my email address?

To change your email, go to Account Settings and select "Change Email." You'll receive a verification email at both your current and new address for security.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. For security, the link expires after 1 hour.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion from Account Settings. Note: this action is permanent and will delete all your progress and data. We'll keep your email for a 30-day grace period in case you change your mind.

How do I manage notification preferences?

Visit your Profile page and scroll to "Notification Preferences." You can toggle email notifications, daily reminders, weekly summaries, and goal deadline alerts.

üìä Tracking Progress

How do I track my progress?

Your Dashboard shows a visual overview of all your active roles with progress bars. You can click into each role to see detailed progress by stage. The progress calendar also displays your activity over the last 30 days.

What do the badges and levels mean?

Badges represent milestones and achievements (e.g., "First Task Completed," "Week Streak"). Levels increase as you earn XP by completing tasks. Your level reflects your overall engagement and progress on the platform.

How is my streak calculated?

Your streak counts consecutive days on which you completed at least one task. If you miss a day, your streak resets to 0, but you can start a new one the next day. Streaks motivate consistent learning!

Can I export my progress?

Yes! From your Profile page, you can export your progress as a CSV or PDF. This is useful for sharing achievements with mentors, employers, or for personal records.

How do I set goals on Pathways?

Go to your Profile page and scroll to "Your Goals." Click "+ Add Goal" to create a short-term or long-term goal with a description and target date. Goals help keep you motivated and organized.

ü§ñ AI Assistant

What can the AI Assistant help me with?

The AI Assistant (powered by Gemini) can answer career questions, generate learning plans, explain skills and concepts, recommend career paths based on your interests, and compare different careers to help you decide.

How do I use the AI Assistant?

Visit the "AI Assistant" page from the main navigation. Choose a task type (Q&A, 6-Week Plan, Skill Explainer, etc.), and type your question in the chat. The AI will respond with personalized guidance.

Is there a limit to how many questions I can ask?

No! You can ask unlimited questions. However, very long conversations may be truncated for performance. If needed, start a new conversation or export important responses.

üìö Learning Resources

Where do I find learning resources?

Visit the "Learn Library" from the main navigation. Resources are curated and organized by category (Videos, Articles, Courses, Tools). You can also find role-specific resources by viewing a particular role.

Are the resources free?

Most resources are free or have free tiers. Some premium resources are marked with a "Pro" badge. We carefully curate quality content that offers great value at all price points.

Can I suggest new resources?

Absolutely! Visit the Forum or email us with your suggestions. Our team reviews submissions and adds high-quality resources regularly. Credit is given to contributors.
